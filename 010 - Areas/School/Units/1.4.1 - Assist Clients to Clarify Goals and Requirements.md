## 1.4.1.1 - Explain clearly to clients the policy on record keeping and confidentiality
### Intended Learning Outcomes
- Access organisational policy on record-keeping and confidentiality requirements
- Read and understand record-keeping and confidentiality policy as it applies to them and to all others who use the workplace
- Provide full details of record-keeping and confidentiality policy to the client, to ensure they understand how information is to be obtained and handled.

## 1.4.1.2. Encourage clients to identify and explore their aims, requirements and ideas
### Intended Learning Outcomes
- Give the client time to talk and speak freely about their issues and/or problems
- Use questioning techniques to help determine the client’s situation and goals
- Help the client to state their goals in positive terms
- Sculpt client goals when it is necessary to assist the client in forming positive goals.

## 1.4.1.3. Assist clients to identify practical goals and requirements for these, and discuss with clients
### Indended Learning Outcomes
- Assist the client in formulating achievable goals 
- Ensure client goals are specific, measurable, realistic and time-based
- Provide information and guidance on how the client can reach their goals
- Adapt client goals to suit the needs of the client as they progress through counselling.

## 1.4.1.4 Identify situations where aims and requirements of clients cannot be met, and make referrals to
### Intended Learning OUtcomes
- Recognise when a client requires additional guidance and support which is out of their own work role and area of responsibility
- Determine client concerns of a serious nature which require referral to other services
- Follow organisational policy and procedures for making client referrals.
