 *How might temperature impact pasture growth?*
 ~
 Evaporation/evapotranspiration = less water for plant growth
 <10c = reduced growth
 <6c = mostly stopped

# Average Daily Minimum Temperature in Australia
*Describe the trend in temperature across australia*
