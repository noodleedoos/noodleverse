# Histology
*Describe the histology of elastic fibres*
~
- Eosinophillic in H&E
- Cannot distinguish from collagen/reticular in H&E - Black in Verhoeff's stain
- Yellow fibrous tissue
- Branched fibres, thinner than collagen
