# Location
~ 
- origin on frontal, parietal, temporal and occipital bones (lateral surface of cranium)
- Insertion on coronoid process of mandible

# Function
~
- Contraction pulls mandible up

# Species Variation
~
Large in carnivores, small in herbivores
