*What is the temperature range for dogs, cats, horses, sheep and dairy cattle?*
~
Cat - 38
Dog - 38.5
Honse - 37.5
Sheep - 39
Dairy cattle - 38.5
Beef cattle - 37 +-2
Beef cattle < Honse < cat < dairy dog < sheep
